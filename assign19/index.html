The In-Between Game

Please enter your name:








Rules to be followed:

1. Player starts with $50 Credit to start the game.
2. Two random cards will be shown.
3. Player will bet the amount they want to, by seeing the shown cards and possibilities of next card being in between them.
4. Players can bet $0 if they think the next card will not be in between the two shown cards.
5. If the next card is in between the two shown cards, player wins the bet, and money is added to player's balance.
6. If the next card is not in between the two shown cards, player will loose the bet and money will be deducted from player's balance.

OBJECT OF THE GAME

The goal is to be the player with the most money at the end of the game.







RANK OF CARDS

A (high), K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2
